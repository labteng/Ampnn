About Us

The Music Psychology and Neuroscience Network brings together scientists, clinicians, educators, industry professionals, and students from diverse fields including neuroscience, psychology, music, cognitive science, computer science, artificial intelligence, and medicine. We advance the understanding of music’s impacts on the brain, cognition, and well-being through interdisciplinary collaboration, education, and public engagement.

Members

Our members include researchers, educators, professionals, and students from neuroscience, psychology, music, cognitive science, computer science, artificial intelligence, and medicine.

Our Mission

To promote scientific knowledge of music’s role in cognitive function, emotional well-being, and neurological health, and to communicate this knowledge through research, education, and outreach across Asia.

Welcome to Our Community

Bringing together diverse perspectives to advance knowledge and understanding in music neuroscience and psychology.

Research Highlights

Explore recent studies and breakthroughs in music cognition, auditory neuroscience, and music-based therapy. Our members contribute to leading journals and conferences, pushing forward the boundaries of how we understand the relationship between music and the brain.
