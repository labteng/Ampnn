About Us

The Music Psychology and Neuroscience Network brings together scientists, clinicians, educators, industry professionals, and students from diverse fields including neuroscience, psychology, music, cognitive science, computer science, artificial intelligence, and medicine. Our goal is to advance the understanding of musicâ€™s profound impacts on the human brain, well-being, and cognitive development, with practical extensions into clinical and technological applications. We achieve this by facilitating research collaborations, providing educational resources, and organizing events such as workshops, seminars, and conferences across Asia. By promoting public awareness and encouraging knowledge exchange, AMPNN fosters a vibrant interdisciplinary community committed to uncovering the therapeutic, educational, and cognitive benefits of music.

Members

Our members include researchers, educators, industry professionals, and students from diverse fields including neuroscience, psychology, music, cognitive science, computer science, artificial intelligence, and medicine.

Our Mission

To enhance scientific knowledge of music's role in cognitive function, emotional well-being, and neurological health, and to effectively communicate this knowledge to the public through interdisciplinary research, education, collaboration, and outreach across Asia.
