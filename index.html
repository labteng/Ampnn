About Us

The Music Psychology and Neuroscience Network brings together scientists, clinicians, educators, and students from diverse fields including neuroscience, psychology, music, AI, and medicine. We advance understanding of music’s effects on the brain, cognition, and well-being through research, collaboration, and public engagement.

Members

Our members include researchers, educators, and professionals from neuroscience, psychology, music, and related fields across Asia.

Our Mission

To expand scientific knowledge of music’s role in cognition, emotion, and neurological health—and to share these insights through interdisciplinary research, education, and outreach across Asia.
